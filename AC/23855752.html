MoSCow PriorityS / C

Supporting Context

Any files on the F drive are not available to the student as soon as their status is changed to interrupted.  Files placed on the F drive are only accessible to the student and certain ITS administration accounts.

Granting access to this file store, while switching off all other data and system access is not easily achievable.  The student's 365 account provides enough storage (1 TB) to hold F: drive (1 GB) files,  

The following document indicates there have been 24 requests for access between August 2014 and December 2015 (not all of these may have been for interrupted students).  The case descriptions, given in the document indicate Service Desk have usually granted temporary access where this is approved by School representatives. 

Is a delay in setting the student status to interrupted, with the provision of an interrupting guide to the transfer of files by the student, and support provided in case of difficulty an option? - The benefit of this is an increased contact with the university..
