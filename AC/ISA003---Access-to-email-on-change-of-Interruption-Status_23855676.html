Accman : ISA003 - Access to email on change of Interruption Status

Created by Jeremy Sugden, last modified by Julia Kelbrick on Dec 24, 2015

User Story

Prefix
Detail
As a <Actor>Purchasing Officer
I want <Need>The email account activity/type to be changed to that corresponding to the new student and registration statuses on Banner.
So that <Reason>The university follows its policy on allocation of email accounts and stays within the university's licence agreement
JIRA 
MoSCow Priority M

Supporting Context

The university has a number of different states of student and registration.  The combination of these informs the statuses in Accman, which are used to determine what happens to a students email account.  These relationships remain the same, apart for the change for students with a Student and Registration status of 'IN'.  when a student changes status from IN this must produce the current outcome of that status combination.
