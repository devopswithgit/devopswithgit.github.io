Accman : Deletion of Old Records

SummaryDeletion of Old Records 
Issue TypeEnhancement
PriorityMedium
Raised ByPaul Daniels
Date Raised20 Apr 2015 
SourcePaul Daniels 
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-191 Open  

 

Details

There is no process inplace to remove old records and a number of tables are continually growing and slowing down access, e.g. transactions, transaction_params.

Comments:

Initial thoughts are to start to mark accounts to the existing account status of 'deletable' once the current selection criteria becomes true (This criteria can be confirmed with Paul Daniels) but its is along the lines of account state = disable, disablement has occurred in the last 6 months and person state is not present nor absent.

This could also set an AD attribute / fag to indicate to Operations that the account can be processed by them.

A subsequent requirement would be investigate a mechanism for checking that Operations have executed the deletion and then set the account state to deleted
