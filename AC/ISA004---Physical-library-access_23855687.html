Accman : ISA004 - Physical library access

User Story

Prefix

JIRA 
MoSCow PriorityM - The library must have access to a Banner enquiry screen that gives access to Student and Registration Status

Supporting Context

 The student ID card will continue to be disabled, removing physical access rights, borrowing rights and access to all electronic resources, in line with the current processing of interrupting students. A manual process to issue cards to External Borrowers,will be used to provide an access card via the Janus system for interrupting students on their first visit to the library. It is thought that a minority of the 600 students who interrupt each year will use this facility. The additional step in the External Borrower process for interrupted students will be to check on Banner to ensure they have a registration status of ‘IN’.  This information is on the enquiry screen SYSTUDQ, which is accessible to library staff. No other check is needed at this point, but the student’s term time address and permanent address, held by Banner, could be confirmed as current on the enquiry screen SPAIDEN.
