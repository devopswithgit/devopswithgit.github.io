Accman currently provisions Office 365 accounts for students only. Staff (and some types of Associates) are given on-premise Exchange mailboxes.

In 2018, the university is moving towards using Office 365 for all accounts. Exchange will be decomissioned. This will involve some changes to Accman, which is responsible for the following items:

Dynamic Groups

Exchange currently has oround 140 dynamic distribution email groups defined for emailing students and staff en-masse. They are dynamic in that membership is determined by LDAP query "filters". Each group has its own filter, and if a staff member or student is returned in the results, they are a mamber of that group. Here's an example:

Group NameFilter
ESPaCH-All-UG-Students(&(!cn=SystemMailbox{*})(&(&(&(|(&(objectCategory=person)(objectSid=*)(!samAccountType:1.2.840.113556.1.4.804:=3))(&(objectCategory=person)(!objectSid=*))(&(objectCategory=group)(groupType:1.2.840.113556.1.4.804:=14))))(objectCategory=contact)(physicalDeliveryOfficeName=School of English, Sociology, Politics and Contemporary History))))

This is a group that allows lecturers in the school of ESPaCH (English, Sociology, Politics & Contemporary History) to email all of their students. When an academic sends an email to the ESPaCH-All-UG-Students group, any student returned by the query/filter above will receive the email. Being dynamic, lecturers don't have to worry about maintaining the list of students in that group when they arrive/leave, because Accman automatically sets the properties in AD that ensure they will be returned by the query. 

In the new implementation of Office365, the groups will be defined in Office365 instead. It will have groups such as ESPaCH-All-UG-Students, but they wont be dynamic though, so Accman will need to determine whether a new account should belong to each group and then add them. The proposed solution to do this is as follows:

After a new AD account has been provisioned, retrieve a list of all dynamic groups and their filters. This list will be stored in the Accman database where it can be maintained. Accman will then loop through each of the groups and execute the filter. If the newly provisioned account is returned in the results, Accman will then fire off an instruction to add them to the corresponding group in Office365. Adding them will be done via a sepatate powershell-based transaction.
