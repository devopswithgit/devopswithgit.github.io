Accman : CohortRescue2 SVN Branch

Created by Julia Kelbrick, last modified on Jan 10, 2013
Contains the following modifications completed on 10/01/2013:

 

  • Modification in response to ITBM Case 242283.  It was taking a long time for the "Manage Associate Cohorts" and "Associate Bulk Upload" forms to open and to return from in AccMan Front End. 

  Each of these operations are currently taking 14 minutes to complete on the production system (this is even without changing any data) and the more cohorts and associates that exist, the longer this will take in the future.  

  Rather than just loading the cohort-level data it was also loading data about all the associated associates. 

  The purpose of this modification is to reduce the time taken to access these two forms in AccMan Front End.

  The AccManLibrary\Models\MySQL\CohortDAO.GetCohorts() method has been modified to stop it retrieving this unnecessary data.

  To Test

  1.  The time taken between selecting the "Manage Associate Cohorts" link and the page appearing was around a quarter of an hour.  It should now be almost immediate.

  2.  The time taken between selecting the "Associate Bulk Upload" link and the page appearing was around a quarter of an hour.  It should now be almost immediate.

  3.  A general regression test of the Manage Associate Cohorts and Bulk Upload functionality in AccManFE to ensure that the modification has not broken anything.

 

 

NB:  This modification is also contained in the "CohortRescue" Subversion Branch and the proposed "5 Fixes" release.  However, due to delays in deploying this release and due to the urgency of this particular fix, this modification has been redone for an AccMan release which is due to be released Jan 2013.
