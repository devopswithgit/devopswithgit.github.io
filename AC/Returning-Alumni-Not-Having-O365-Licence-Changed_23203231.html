Accman : Returning Alumni Not Having O365 Licence Changed

Created by Paul Daniels, last modified by Jon Besson on Dec 04, 2015
SummaryReturning Alumni that have a Alumnus Licence are not reverted to a student licence
Issue TypeBug
PriorityHigh
Raised ByPaul Daniels
Date Raised18 Nov 2015 
Source 
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-291 Resolved

 

Details

Due to RFC2168, students that become alumnus are having their O365 account changed to an Alumnus licence. However there is no provision in Accman for setting the account back to a student licence, e.g. returning alumnus. This has raised cases in the past.
