We have a number of scripts we use to check the state of student and staff accounts in Q:\Account Manager\support\useradmin\, used for diagnostics:

staffquery.pl                      Report of staff SAP and Accman state.

mailchange.pl                    Change the email on an account.

t999_xxxxxxx.pl               Various scripts to generate transactions.

reconcile.pl                         Reconcile two accounts.

unreconciled.pl                 Unreconcile two accounts.

generate_Type_48_and_Type_39_transactions.pl           Generate transactions to generate password.txt and O365 account.

extend.pl                            Extend and account.

 

We should find a way of replacing the perl or automating the process.
