There will be 3 sub categories at this location - Review (needs to be reviewed), Pending (has agreed to be done as a work package, and has a JIRA issue associated with it), Completed (work completed, JIRA issue closed), Won't Fix (won't fix)

 

A proposed triage process could be :

When creating an issue, the issue should be first put into the Review section.

Once a week, a meeting is convened to discuss outstanding AccMan Issues. Issues in the review section are looked at, and any that are deemed not to be fixed are put into the won't fix area. Issues that are deemed to require fixes will be put into the Pending area.

Once an issue is pending, it should have a JIRA issue raised against it. The JIRA issue should have a fix version associated with it. This JIRA issue should be referenced in the template of the Confluence page.

Once the JIRA issue has been released to production, the Confluence page can be moved to the 'Completed' area.
