Accman : Kibana

Created by Unknown User (aaa45815), last modified on May 16, 2016

Kibana provides a front end to the data that was pushed into ElasticSearch for querying and visualising. We can customise it with dashboards to report on potential issues and also provide calculated fields to provide funtionality that would allow us to do things such as link to an account in AccMan and link to actual log entries against a transaction. With the full text search capabilities we could quite easily just enter a username and find all log/transaction entries that mention that username.
