Details

There are a number of areas that logging can be improved:

  • Reduce the unneccessary entries that are of no use, e.g. trace dumps.
  • Make sure every log is time stamped
  • Make sure every log is descriptive and refers to whatever had a problem, e.g. account id.
  • Remove the errors being sent to the console window as they just scroll off the screen and lost.
