Introduction

Associates go through their own creation process, starting with the Associate Request form available via the main university website (somewhere). The URL is https://accmanweb.salford.ac.uk/AssociateRequest. A member of staff (the Sponsor) enters the details of the new associate on this form, and the details are emailed to Service Desk who will create the account using Accman.

Basic Process

Emails

Complete Associate Generation

Email Content

Your request to set up an account for [associate_title] [associate_name] has been processed.

The user name for the account is: [associate_username]

Prior to using the account it must be activated. Instructions on how to do this are below.

Password Format - General Case (applies to most people)
The password will be the first three letters of the user's surname (with the initial letter in upper case and the others in lower case) followed by the last five digits of their contact telephone number, followed by the letter X (uppercase).

Password Format - Exceptions
Please bear in mind the following additional rules

  • If their surname has exactly three letters, their password will be the first two letters of their surname, followed by the last six digits of their contact phone number, followed by one 'X'
  • If their surname has fewer than three letters, their password will be their surname plus the last six digits of their contact telephone number, followed by as many 'X's as required to make it up to nine characters in length.
  • If the user's surname contains an apostrophe then this is ignored entirely.
  • If the surname only contains one letter, it will be converted to lowercase.

You now need to give [associate_title] [associate_name] access to https://identity.salford.ac.uk/AccManSelfActFE so that they can enable their account.

As part of the enablement process, they will have to provide answers to a number of security questions. These answers will subsequently be used to enable them to prove their identity in circumstances such as when they have forgotten their password and need to reset it.

Once [associate_title] [associate_name] has enabled their account, they will be able to access the University of Salford network immediately and their other University of Salford resources after one hour.

If you do not understand any of the information in this email or you require further information, please contact the Digital IT Service Desk by emailing Digital-ITServicedesk@salford.ac.uk. or by telephoning +44 (0)161 295 2444 during office hours Monday to Friday 0800 - 1700.



Activated Complete Generation

Email Content

Your request to set up an account for [associate_title] [associate_name] has been processed.

The user name for the account is: [associate_username]

This account will be activated automatically on the start date.

The password will be the first three letters of the user's surname (with the initial letter in upper case and the others in lower case) plus the last five digits of their contact telephone number.

For example:

For user Mr. John Smith with contact telephone number 07951 345432, the password will be: Smi45432

If you do not understand any of the information in this email or you require further information, please contact the ITS Service Desk by emailing its-servicedesk@salford.ac.uk. or by telephoning +44 (0)161 295 2444 during office hours Monday to Friday 0800 - 1700.

[N.B. If their surname comprises only two letters, their password will be their surname plus the last six digits of their contact telephone number and if their surname comprises only one letter, their password will be 'p' plus their surname plus the last six digits of their contact telephone number. If the user's surname contains an apostrophe then this is ignored entirely.]

Associate Reminder (sent to associate)

Email Content

[associate_username], [associate_title] [associate_name] Your association with the University Of Salford is due to end on [associate_end_date]. If no further action is taken, after this date, you will no longer be able to use the University Of Salford IT resources.

If you wish to extend your association with the University contact your sponsor or if you require further information, please contact the ITS Service Desk by emailing its-servicedesk@salford.ac.uk or by telephoning +44 (0)161 295 2444 during office hours Monday to Friday 0800 - 1700.

Associate Reminder (sent to sponsor)

Email Content

The association of user [associate_username], [associate_title] [associate_name] with the University Of Salford is due to end on [associate_end_date]. If no further action is taken, after this date, this user will no longer be able to use their University Of Salford IT resources.

You are currently this user's sponsor. If you wish to extend this user's association with the University or you require further information, please contact the ITS Service Desk by emailing its-servicedesk@salford.ac.uk or by telephoning +44 (0)161 295 2444 during office hours Monday to Friday 0800 - 1700.
