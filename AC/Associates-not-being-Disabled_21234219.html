Accman : Associates not being Disabled

Created by Paul Daniels, last modified by Stephen Harrison on Nov 17, 2015
SummaryAssociates not being Disabled
Issue TypeBug
PriorityHigh
Raised ByPaul Daniels
Date Raised14 May 2015 
SourcePaul Daniels
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-241 Resolved

 

Details

Associates are not always being disabled after they have reached their associate end date. See V:\ITS\Programme Management\Applications\Support\Applications\Accman v2.0\Undeleted Unused AD Accounts Research.xlsx, worksheet "Assoc - Not Disabled". There is another worksheet, "Assoc - To Be Disabled" that have not been disabled by the system but will be disabled manually as part of a tiding up exercise.

It appears that the current logic used in the script, which is run nightly, only checks for associates that ended that day. If the script fails to run, associates ending on that day are never disabled.

 

A case has come in specifically for AAA27884 who wasn't disabled on Wednesday 1st April. There are 56 associates with an end date of 01-04-2015 that are valid and 15 that are disabled.
