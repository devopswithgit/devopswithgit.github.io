Accman : RFID Numbers not always sent to AD

Created by Martin Evans, last modified on Feb 23, 2016
SummaryRFID Numbers not always sent to AD
Issue TypeBug
PriorityHigh
Raised ByMartin Evans
Date Raised23 Feb 2016 
SourceMartin Evans
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-338 - RFID Numbers Not always sent to AD Pending

 

Details

  • A user's 32 and 56-bit numbers in Janus do not always push through to AD, resulting in being unable to log into the printers.
  • I have not found a pattern to this when they do or don't update.
  • Manual fix is to change the number to something else and change it back - it then triggers an update to AD.

  • Examples where a manual update were required are: MUE840, LZD715, AYB028, NUS683, BTP146, ESS347, PPS335, LAE929
