Accman : Person States

Created by Unknown User (uis894), last modified on Sep 02, 2016

Introduction

Accman has the concept of a 'Person State' which is the current status of the person i.e. are they physically here at the university, are they not here yet but expected to arrive next month etc etc.

These apply to staff, students and associates. A person's state can change for many reasons e.g. when data from Banner changes or when a Service Desk user disables an account for some reason.

Accman has the following statuses.Click on the status name for more details on each one.

StateMeaning
UnknownAccman doesn't know the person's current state

Expected

Not present, but they are expected to arrive in the future
PresentCurrently at the university
GraduatingStudent has finished their studies and waiting for the graduation ceremony
AbsentShould be here but are not for some reason e.g. long-term illness.
LeftNo longer at the university e.g. they have resigned (staff) or finished their degree (students)
MissingShould be here but are not and the reason is not known
DeletedUsed to mark old accounts as candidates for deletion
ExtendedUser would normally have left (e.g. fixed-term contract is up) but they have agreed to stay with the university for a bit longer
