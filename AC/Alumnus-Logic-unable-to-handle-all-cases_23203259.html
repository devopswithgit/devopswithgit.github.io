Accman : Alumnus Logic unable to handle all cases

Created by Paul Daniels, last modified by Jon Besson on Dec 04, 2015
SummaryData in Banner shows that students do not have a graduating date for the same term code they completed their course
Issue TypeBug
PriorityMedium
Raised ByPaul Daniels
Date Raised19 Nov 2015 
Source 
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-292 Open

 

Details

It is possible for the Graduation date not to be entered in the same the year that a student completes their programme.

ID                  First name    Last name    Start Term    DEGS    Completion Term    Grad Term    Grad Date

@00264549    Sarah          Harper         201110         AW       201310                  201610        16/11/2015

This student completed their programme in 201310 and however has a graduation date of 16/11/2015. Due to the gap it is unlikely we will still be importing this students record are part of Banner4Import.

Therefore the Alumnus will never see a record for this student with both an Awarded state and Graduation date.
