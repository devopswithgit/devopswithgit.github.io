Supporting Context

 The student ID card will continue to be disabled, removing physical access rights, borrowing rights and access to all electronic resources, in line with the current processing of interrupting students. The library access card will be used to provide reduced borrowing rights  via Alma. Access to electronic resources will be managed by a member of staff physically logging on to a library pc with limited service on behalf of the interrupted student. This requirement is wholly out of scope for the ITS solution.
