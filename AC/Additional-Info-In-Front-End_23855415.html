Details

  • There is a need to provide additional information in the UI to support resolution / explanation of queries for Accman accounts, for example:
    • For students it would be good to have visibility of their end date, graduation date, banner status, last banner term etc.
    • For students it would be good to see what office365 license they had and their card status in Janus 

Comments:

In conjunction with some of the other requirements we need to start to display simple predictive / progress style information such as:

 

If the state is disabled / left then the account is due for deletion on dd/mm/yyyy

If the state is valid / graduating then the account is due to changes state on dd/mm/yyy (i.e. when the graduation date is in the past)
