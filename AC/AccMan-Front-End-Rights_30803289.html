Operator Levels

Summary

The ability to connect to the Accman Frontend is controlled by the Operators table. There are 9 levels with 2 being the most frequently used for Service Desk agents.

All permissions will be shown as a table of levels with green tick indicating an allowed level and red cross indicating a disallowed level e.g. the following indicates that all users except level 7 are allowed to use an operation:
