Accman : Unactivated Associate Accounts being Disabled

Created by Paul Daniels, last modified on Mar 10, 2016
SummaryAssociate accounts that do not get activated are still being disabled on their end date.
Issue TypeBug

SourcePaul Daniels 
JIRA Issue(s) ARF-342 - Unactivated Associate Accounts being Disabled Closed

 

Details

When an associate comes to their end date, Accman will attempt to disable it. However if the account was never activated the disable process is still run. Currently this is resulting in tr 104s failing and being left in a pending state. Two accounts this has happened to recently are AAA28428 and AAA30436.

Expecting Accman to not disable accounts that have not been activated. This might be true for unused staff acccounts too.
